About Me

My name is Farzan, pronounced /f uh r-z aa n/. I've grown up all my life mispronouncing it incorrectly myself, so don't worry too much about the pronounciation! This is primarily a reminder for me to pronounce my own name correctly. The name originates from Persia and means wisdom. I don't know how accurately that reflects who I am, but I am someone who loves to learn.

I am a 3rd year Honours Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo and pursuing a minor in Combinatorics/Optimization. I recently was a Software Engineer Intern at Rodan Energy Solutions, developing software for sustainable energy. I previously interned at NCR as a Backend SWE, Questrade as a Full Stack Engineer on the core systems and data platform team, and a small startup in high-school building a social web app for sharing recipes.
